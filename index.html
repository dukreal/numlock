About NumLock

The Game

NumLock is a strategic puzzle game that combines probability with tactical decision-making. Every roll matters, and one wrong placement can determine your fate.

How It Works

Goal: Fill all slots with numbers in strictly ascending order.

Roll the dice, choose where to place your number wisely—once placed, numbers cannot be moved. If you run out of valid positions, it's game over.

Strategy Tips

  • Save high numbers for the rightmost slots
  • Low rolls early = more flexibility later
  • Use skips strategically—they're limited
